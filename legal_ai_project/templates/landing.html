Platform Features

Everything for AI Legal Analysis

Case Analyzer
Upload Supreme Court PDFs or paste judgment text. Extracts parties, sections, outcome, and category using a rule-based ML pipeline with AI correction.
Lincoln Lawyer
Standalone AI legal assistant for Indian law. Ask about procedures, fundamental rights, bail, appeals, and Supreme Court judgments — no case upload needed.
ML Outcome Prediction
Logistic Regression on InLegalBERT embeddings predicts Allowed or Dismissed with a calibrated confidence score. Finds top-5 similar precedents via cosine similarity.
AI Field Correction
Single Cohere call corrects garbled extraction fields and generates a plain-English case summary. Runs only when rule-based extraction detects issues.
Case Context Chatbot
After analysis, ask for risk assessment, next steps, legal arguments, ELI5 explanations, or comparisons — the chatbot has full case context from the session.
Role-Based Access
Admin-approved accounts with granular roles. Staff can approve users, assign Case Analyzer or Lincoln Lawyer access, and toggle AI services live.
Under the Hood

How the Pipeline Works

01
Upload
PDF via pdfminer / pdfplumber, or raw text paste. Document validated as a real court judgment before processing.
›
02
Extract
Rule-based extraction of parties, date, case number, 40+ legal sections, outcome, and category from the judgment text.
›
03
AI Correct
Single Cohere call fixes any extraction errors and generates a plain-English summary. Only fires when issues are detected.
›
04
ML Predict
InLegalBERT encodes the case text into 768-dim embeddings. Logistic Regression predicts outcome with confidence score.
›
05
Results
Full analysis displayed with similar cases, ML insights, and the Lincoln Lawyer chatbot pre-loaded with case context.
